Directory: HArDCore2D/src/DDRCore

The DDR sequence implemented here is based on the construction described in

  Fully discrete polynomial de Rham sequences of arbitrary degree on polygons and polyhedra.
  D. A. Di Pietro, J. Droniou, and F. Rapetti. 
  Math. Models Methods Appl. Sci. 30 (9), pp. 1809–1855, 2020. 
  doi: 10.1142/S0218202520500372. url: https://arxiv.org/abs/1911.03616.

  An arbitrary-order discrete de Rham complex on polyhedral meshes: Exactness, Poincaré inequalities, and consistency. 
  D. A. Di Pietro and J. Droniou. Found. Comput. Math. 80p, 2021. 
  doi: 10.1007/s10208-021-09542-8. url: https://arxiv.org/abs/2101.04940.


If you use this sequence for a scientific publication, please mention the above articles.
