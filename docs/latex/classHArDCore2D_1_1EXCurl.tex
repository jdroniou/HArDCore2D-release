\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Extended \mbox{\hyperlink{classHArDCore2D_1_1XCurl}{X\+Curl}} space, with vector-\/valued polynomials on the edges. 

Following \mbox{\hyperlink{classHArDCore2D_1_1DDRSpace}{D\+D\+R\+Space}}, the D\+O\+Fs are organised this way\+: D\+O\+Fs of edges (for each edge\+: normal components, then tangential components -\/ both oriented according to the intrinsic normal and tangent to the edge), D\+O\+Fs of cells. The matrices m\+\_\+reduction are used to locally recover D\+O\+Fs for the \mbox{\hyperlink{classHArDCore2D_1_1XCurl}{X\+Curl}} space by removing the normal components to the edges. Extended discrete Hcurl space\+: local operators, L2 product and global interpolator
