\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename Basis\+Type$>$\newline
class HAr\+DCore2\+D\+::\+Hessian\+Basis$<$ Basis\+Type $>$}

Basis for the space of Hessians of polynomials. 

To construct a basis of H$^\wedge$k, this assumes that the scalar basis it is constructed from is an ancestor basis of P$^\wedge$\{k+2\}/\+P$^\wedge$1, space of polynomials of degree k+2 without trivial polynomials with zero Hessian. This can also be used to create a family of Hessians (not necessarily linearly independent, if the ancestor basis is not a basis of P$^\wedge$\{k+2\}/\+P$^\wedge$1)
