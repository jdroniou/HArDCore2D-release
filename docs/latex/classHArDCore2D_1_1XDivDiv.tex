\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Discrete Hdivdiv space. 

At each vertex, the D\+O\+Fs are the coefficients on the basis \char`\"{}m\+\_\+basis\char`\"{} (accessible via m\+\_\+symm\+\_\+vertex) of Symm. On each edge $E$, the D\+O\+Fs are $\tau_E$ on $P^{k-3}(E)$, then $D_{E,\tau}$ on $P^{k-2}(E)$. In each element $T$, the D\+O\+Fs are $\tau_{\mathcal H,T}^c$ in $\mathcal H^{c,k-1}(T)$, and then (if it exists) $\tau_{\mathcal H,T}$ in $\mathcal H^{k-4}(T)$.
